Site Intent

The overall objective to From The Ashes Media is to offer services that musicians look for all the time, but now in one place. By providing musicians a place to record their music, shoot a music video, get album artwork all in a one-stop shop.

From The Ashes is a parent company to Palace of Sorrows recording Studio, and partnered up with Tackleboxxdesigns to provide the best modern day graphics and web work for the people who need it the most.

Palace of Sorrows Studio has worked with several recording artists, (locally and nationally, as well as From The Ashes media which just finished a DVD for Famed Guitarist John 5 (Rob Zombie/ Marilyn Manson) as well as other videos for the Zombie Camp.

From The Ashes will utilize 6 web pages to dial in the specific areas that they want to be known for. About, palace of sorrow, tackleboxxdesigns, videos, Gallery, and Contact. With this being the main focus we can deliver all the necessary goods and quality needs to gain all aspects of the music industry and musicians.
